# Step 1 - Customer Research & Personas

## Your Mission
Use this prompt in ChatGPT/Claude:

"Create 3 user personas for [your product]. Include: job title, daily workflow, psychological drivers, decision-making process, biggest frustrations, and secret motivations. Make them feel like real humans I could interview tomorrow."

## Follow-up Prompts
- "What jobs-to-be-done does each persona have?"
- "Which product features align with each job-to-be-done?"

**Deliverable**: 3 personas + JTBD mapping + feature ideas
**Timer**: 10 minutes - GO!
